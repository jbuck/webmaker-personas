Colene

Educational Funder

Colene is a busy senior executive at one of the world's largest philanthropic organizations. She wants to see the money in her portfolio have maximum impact for the public interest, and she believes that Mozilla's brand and Silicon Valley bonafides are a secret weapon in re-imaging education in the 21st century. She wants to quickly see something that is "of the moment" and approachable that will encourage people around the world to be demonstrably more web literate.
