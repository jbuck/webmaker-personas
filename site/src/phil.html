Phil

Mainstream Educator

Phil is a technology coordinator for a K-12 private school. He wants to adopt a school-wide digital literacy program, but he’s struggling with how to actually implement it. How can he help his time-starved fellow teachers get started? Phil is a passionate and idealistic educator, and he loves Mozilla’s open ethos and emphasis on creativity and collaboration. But he’s also ruthlessly pragmatic – he needs turnkey solutions that work in the real world for busy colleagues and for real hormone-addled middle school students with lots of other things on their mind.
